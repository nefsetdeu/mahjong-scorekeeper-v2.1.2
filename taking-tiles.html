Taking Tiles

After opening the gate, players take turns drawing tiles to form their starting hands.

Drawing Process:

  1. Once the starting point has been identified, the Banker starts the deal by taking four tiles (two stacks of two) immediately beyond the break point.
  2. Each of the other players then takes four tiles in turn (South, West, North).
  3. This continues for a second round of four tiles each, and then a third round of four tiles each.
  4. Finally, East takes two more tiles, and the other players take one more tile each.

Important Note:

The walls are dismantled in a clockwise direction, while players take turns going counter-clockwise (East → South → West → North).

Final Hand Structure:

After drawing, each player should have 13 tiles, except for East who has 14 tiles and will discard one to begin the game.
