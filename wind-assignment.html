Wind Assignment

After determining the Starting Banker based on the dice roll, assign wind positions to all players.

Wind Positions:

  • The Starting Banker becomes East (東).
  • The player to their right becomes South (南).
  • The player across from East becomes West (西).
  • The player to East's left becomes North (北).

First Round:

In the first hand of the game, the Starting Banker is known as Double East (i.e., East round, East seat).

Important Note:

These wind positions determine the seating arrangement for the first round. In later rounds, the winds will rotate among the players, but the physical seating positions remain the same.
