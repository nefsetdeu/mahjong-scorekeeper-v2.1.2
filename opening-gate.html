Opening the Gate

After determining the Starting Banker, it's time to open the gate to begin drawing tiles.

Opening Process:

  1. The Banker throws the dice again to determine where to "break the wall".
  2. Looking at the wall in front of the Banker, and starting from the right-hand side of the stack (inner corner), count the number of tile stacks to the left - using the same number shown on the dice.
  3. This position is where the Banker will "open the gate" and begin drawing tiles.

Example:

If the dice shows 5 and 2 (total: 7), the Banker counts 7 stacks from the right end of the wall in front of them. This position becomes the starting point for drawing tiles.

For Subsequent Hands:

The Banker will always be the one to roll the dice and count around the table, then count the number of tiles to the left.
